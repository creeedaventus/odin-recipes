Chimichurri Sauce

Delicious Chimichurri Sauce!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh parsley
  • ¾ cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 ½ teaspoons minced garlic
  • 1 ½ teaspoons pepper sauce (such as Frank's Red Hot®)
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine parsley, oil, vinegar, oregano, cumin, garlic, hot sauce, and salt in a blender or food processor.
  3. Mix on medium speed until ingredients are evenly blended, about 10 seconds.
  4. Enjoy!
